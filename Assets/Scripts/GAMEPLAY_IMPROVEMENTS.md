# Coffee Shop Gameplay Improvements & Portfolio Enhancements

## üéØ High Priority - Core Gameplay Enhancements

### 1. **Customer Variety & Orders System**
- **Different Customer Types**: Regular, VIP, Rushed, Patient
- **Order Types**: 
  - Simple Coffee (1 bean, fast)
  - Premium Coffee (3 beans, slower)
  - Double Shot (5 beans, very slow)
- **Customer Personalities**: Affect patience, payment amount, and dialogue
- **Visual Variety**: Different customer models/appearances

### 2. **Progression & Upgrade System**
- **Machine Upgrades**: 
  - Faster brewing time
  - Multiple cups per batch
  - Auto-collection feature
- **Player Upgrades**:
  - Increased inventory capacity
  - Faster movement speed
  - Better interaction range
- **Shop System**: Spend money to unlock upgrades

### 3. **Goals & Achievements**
- **Daily Goals**: "Serve 10 customers today"
- **Achievements**: "Serve 100 customers", "Perfect day (no lost customers)", "Speed demon"
- **Milestones**: Unlock new features at certain customer counts

### 4. **Customer Satisfaction System**
- **Rating System**: 1-5 stars based on wait time
- **Tips**: Higher ratings = more tips
- **Loyalty**: Returning customers pay more
- **Combo System**: Serve multiple customers quickly for bonus

### 5. **Time Pressure & Difficulty Scaling**
- **Rush Hours**: Periods with more customers and higher payouts
- **Difficulty Scaling**: More customers spawn as you progress
- **Time Limits**: Optional timed challenges
